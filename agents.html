Agents

Build agents in plain English.

Custom AI helpers, designed and configured without writing code.

Once Bauer is installed, every persona ships with a build-agent skill. Just say “help me build an agent that…” in your terminal, and Bauer walks you through it.

An agent is a small set of instructions that tells your AI exactly what to do, what to access, and what limits to respect. Bauer turns the design process into a conversation.

  • Define what it does. Plain-English purpose, trigger, output. One sentence each.
  • Set careful permissions. Should it read your Drive but never delete? Read your inbox but not reply? Tell Bauer in everyday language.
  • Pick your model. Closed-source (Claude Pro, $20/mo) for the easiest path. Or open-source local (Ollama + Llama or Mistral) for free and private.
  • Set hard guardrails. Rules the agent will never break — like “never send anything without showing me first.”
  • Bauer writes the agent file. You get a trigger phrase, an editable file, and one example prompt to try right away.

What people actually build

  • 📧 Inbox triage — reads new emails every morning, flags the ones that need a real reply
  • 📝 Weekly client updates — takes raw notes, drafts a status email per client
  • 📊 Spreadsheet summarizer — pulls key numbers from a sales spreadsheet into a one-paragraph recap
  • 📅 Meeting prep — on Sunday, drafts talking points for next week’s key meetings
  • 🎙️ Newsletter drafter — turns this week’s notes into a draft post for review

Bauer doesn’t ship these pre-built — but it walks you through making any of them yourself in 5–10 minutes.
